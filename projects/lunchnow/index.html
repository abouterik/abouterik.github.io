Lunchnow

FIND YOUR NEXT LUNCH & MEET WITH FRIENDS

From October 2018 on I started developing an app called ‘LunchNow‘. It’s mainly a service for users to find lunch offers near their places or offices. First this was mainly a complete rework of a previous version made in Java & Objective C (Android and iOS respectively) into a new codebase with Typescript and React Native.


Later on I integrated personalization features like your personal daily suggestions and a whole new feature set to organize personal meetups with your friends or colleagues. Here you could invite other users to a specific restaurant and date you’ve chosen. The invitees then may accept, decline or propose a new date for the meetup.


Year: 2018/2019

*Key tasks: *

  • Backend development with nodeJS & Firebase
  • App development with React Native & Typescript
  • Setup & maintenance of a build pipeline (MS Appcenter)
  • Release management for iOS & Android
  • Cloud- and Virtual Machine Administration
  • Setup, Maintenance and Development of Firebase projects and clients
  • Automatization and monitoring of Firebase infrastructure
  • Definition of rules/needs for product development (IT Governance)
  • Admin for technical infrastructure, software, hardware, development tools etc.
